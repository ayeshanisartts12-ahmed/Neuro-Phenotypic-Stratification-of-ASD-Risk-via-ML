🔭 Project Vision

NeuroStrat AI is not just an academic requirement — it is a mission that reflects a vision for a healthier, more aware, and more supportive Pakistan. The project aims to develop an intelligent AI-assisted triage system that can stratify individuals into clinically relevant ASD risk categories.

✦  By using advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques, the system assists healthcare professionals in accelerating the referral process for formal clinical assessment and early intervention — making that identification faster, more consistent, and more accessible.

As a student, researcher, and future AI engineer, the goal is to create real-world impact rather than remain limited to theory. Even a small technological contribution can help transform lives and spread awareness across the nation.

⚠️ Problem Statement

In Pakistan, awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is still very limited. Many children and teenagers remain undiagnosed because families often do not recognize the early signs of autism, and access to professional clinical assessment is difficult, expensive, or unavailable in many areas.

As a result, countless individuals do not receive timely support and intervention. Current diagnostic processes rely heavily on specialist availability, creating significant bottlenecks in the healthcare system.

🔍
Limited awareness of early ASD signs among families in Pakistan
🏥
Professional clinical assessment is costly & hard to access in many regions
⏳
Delayed diagnosis leads to missed critical windows for early intervention
👨‍👩‍👧
Families lack structured support and guidance at the right time

🧠 AI & Healthcare Mission

While observing these challenges in society, the realization emerged that Artificial Intelligence can play an important role in helping healthcare systems identify autism-related risks earlier and more efficiently.

The platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning techniques to analyze behavioral, vocal, and cognitive data across multiple modalities — producing clinically relevant ASD risk stratification results classified as High Risk, Moderate Risk, or Low Risk.

"The system is designed to bridge the gap between AI research and clinical practice by providing explainable, interpretable outputs that support — not replace — the professional clinical judgment of healthcare providers."

This platform serves as a clinical triage tool, enabling faster identification of high-risk individuals who require immediate specialist attention, while providing peace of mind for lower-risk cases.

⚖️ Ethics & Privacy

This platform is designed with a strong ethical framework to ensure responsible AI in healthcare:

  • All interactions are anonymous at the AI processing layer
  • No raw interaction data leaves the user's session without explicit consent
  • Results are clearly labelled as AI-assisted, not diagnostic
  • HIPAA-inspired data handling protocols are applied throughout
  • The platform includes mandatory consent gates before any screening begins
  • Explainable AI (SHAP) ensures clinical transparency in all risk outputs
⚠ This platform provides AI-assisted behavioral risk stratification and is not a substitute for professional clinical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for formal ASD assessment.
