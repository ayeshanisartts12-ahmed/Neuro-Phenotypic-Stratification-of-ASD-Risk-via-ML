How NeuroStrat AI Works

A step-by-step guide to the ASD screening experience

📋 Screening Process

Step 1 — Create Account & Login

Register with your email address on the login page. Your credentials are securely stored using industry-standard encryption. Existing users simply sign in to access their dashboard and screening history.

Step 2 — Complete Registration Form

Fill in the participant's details: full name, age, gender, country, and optional medical notes. The platform automatically identifies the most appropriate AI interaction module based on the submitted age.

Step 3 — Review AI Module Selection

Review the automatically selected age-appropriate module. You can see a preview of what the interaction will involve. Confirm your selection and proceed to the consent section.

Step 4 — Consent & Privacy Agreement

Read the data collection notice and ethical AI disclaimer carefully. Provide informed consent for data processing. All three consent checkboxes must be accepted before proceeding.

Step 5 — Launch AI Interaction Module

Begin the interactive AI session. Depending on your age group, this may involve gameplay, social storytelling, voice conversation, or cognitive tasks. The AI silently records behavioral metrics in the background.

Step 6 — AI Analysis & Results

Upon completion, the multi-modal AI engine processes all collected data. Within seconds, you receive a full stratification report including risk level, confidence score, and detailed behavioral analysis with clinical recommendations.

🤖 AI Interaction Modules Explained

🎮

Ages 4–8 | Behavioral Adventure Platform

Children engage with a colorful interactive world featuring room decoration tasks, object sorting games, emotion recognition activities, and memory mini-games. The AI analyzes repetitive actions, creativity, emotional understanding, attention span, and reaction speed — all without the child ever knowing they're being assessed.

📖

Ages 9–12 | Social Simulation Platform

Story-based exploration through school, classroom, and park environments. Children make emotional decisions within narrative scenarios (e.g., "How do you respond when a friend is upset?"). The AI evaluates empathy, social reasoning, adaptability, and behavioral consistency.

🎤

Ages 12–16 | Voice Interaction Platform

A futuristic AI assistant poses real-life social questions for users to answer verbally. The voice analysis engine processes speaking confidence, hesitation patterns, communication flow, emotional consistency, and stress indicators in real-time.

💬

Ages 17–22 | Social Reasoning & Voice Confidence

Realistic emotional and professional social scenarios presented for verbal response and reasoning. The AI tracks confidence level, communication fluency, emotional reasoning, hesitation patterns, and conversational consistency in a mature, research-lab style interface.

🧠

Ages 22+ | Cognitive Interaction Platform

Adaptive AI conversations combined with cognitive challenge simulations, context-switching tasks, and multitasking interaction tests. The AI tracks behavioral consistency, attention switching, communication organization, cognitive flexibility, stress response, and adaptability under pressure.
